# ZeusLabs Design Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Logo Usage
- **Primary logo**: Zeus figure with lightning bolt in circular gradient + "ZeusLabs" text
- **Secondary logo**: Compact Zeus logo for icon use and smaller spaces
- **Brand message**: Combines mythological power with modern technology excellence
- **Design elements**: Purple-to-teal gradient background, white line art Zeus figure, lightning bolt
- **Minimum size**: 24px height for digital use (maintains detail visibility)
- **Clear space**: Maintain at least 50% of logo height as clear space around the logo
- **Animation**: Subtle scale, rotation, and glow effects with lightning-inspired hover states

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Electric Blue: #3b82f6 (Primary brand color - represents power and reliability)
- Deep Purple: #8b5cf6 (Secondary accent color)
- Indigo: #6366f1 (Tertiary accent)
- Yellow Accent: #fbbf24 (Lightning accent for energy)

**Supporting Colors:**
- White: #ffffff (Light backgrounds, text on dark)
- Dark Gray: #1f2937 (Dark backgrounds, text on light)
- Success Green: #10b981
- Warning Orange: #f59e0b
- Error Red: #ef4444

### Typography
- **Font Family**: Josefin Sans (Google Fonts)
- **Weights**: Light (300), Normal (400), Medium (500), Semibold (600), Bold (700)
- **Headings**: Use semibold or bold weights
- **Body text**: Use normal weight
- **UI elements**: Use medium weight

## Component Guidelines

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Electric blue gradient background
- **Secondary**: Outlined with electric blue border
- **Hover states**: Subtle scale (1.05) and glow effects
- **Font weight**: Medium (500)
- **Border radius**: 0.625rem (10px)

### Cards
- **Background**: Semi-transparent with backdrop blur
- **Border**: Subtle border with brand colors
- **Hover**: Slight elevation with glow effect
- **Spacing**: Consistent 1.5rem (24px) padding

### Animations
- **Duration**: 0.3-0.6 seconds for interactions
- **Easing**: ease-in-out for smooth transitions
- **Scale**: 1.02-1.05 for subtle hover effects
- **Glow effects**: Use brand colors with low opacity

### Layout
- **Spacing**: Use 8px grid system (multiples of 8)
- **Breakpoints**: Mobile-first responsive design
- **Max width**: 1200px for main content areas
- **Margins**: Consistent spacing using Tailwind spacing scale

## Zeus Logo Animation Guidelines

### Design Philosophy
- **Core concept**: Mythological power meets modern technology - Zeus figure with lightning bolt
- **Color scheme**: Purple-to-teal gradient background with white line art figure
- **Motion style**: Dynamic movements that suggest divine power and technological innovation

### Logo Elements
- **Main Logo**: Zeus figure with lightning bolt in circular gradient + "ZeusLabs" text
- **Icon Logo**: Compact Zeus logo for smaller contexts
- **Background**: Circular purple-to-teal gradient (purple #8b5cf6 to teal #14b8a6)
- **Figure**: White line art Zeus with lightning bolt, classical pose
- **Text**: "ZEUS" prominently displayed in the logo, "ZeusLabs" as brand text
- **Symbolic meaning**: Divine power, lightning (energy/innovation), leadership

### Hover Effects
- **Scale**: Maximum 1.1 for the logo, 1.05 for container
- **Rotation**: Subtle 2-5 degree rotation for dynamic feel
- **Glow**: Lightning-inspired glow effects with purple/blue colors
- **Duration**: 0.3-0.6 seconds with easeInOut
- **Lightning effects**: Subtle particle or glow animations around the logo

### Loading States
- **Rotation**: 180-degree entrance rotation
- **Pulse**: Scale between 1.0 and 1.05 with glow intensity changes
- **Duration**: 2-3 seconds with divine timing
- **Lightning effects**: Radial glow animations suggesting electrical energy

### Interaction Feedback
- **Click**: Brief scale to 0.95 then back to 1.0 with rotation
- **Hover**: Scale increase to 1.1 with glow and subtle rotation
- **Color shifts**: Gradient can shift intensity on interaction
- **Lightning motion**: Subtle electrical glow effects for premium feel
